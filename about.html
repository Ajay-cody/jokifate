About Jokifate Boresha

Jokifate Boresha is a leading agribusiness company dedicated to training and empowering farmers in modern poultry farming techniques. We provide hands-on workshops, expert consultations, and affordable solutions for layers, broilers, and indigenous chickens.

Our Mission

Our mission is to equip communities with practical poultry farming skills, promote food security, and create sustainable income opportunities through training and innovation.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted provider of poultry farming education and support in the region, transforming lives one farm at a time.

Thank You

Thank you for trusting Jokifate Boresha. We look forward to walking with you on your journey to success in poultry farming!
